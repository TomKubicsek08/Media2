Positive Effects of Social Media

Social media allows people to stay connected, share ideas, and learn about new cultures.

It has also helped in raising awareness about important social issues.

Negative Effects of Social Media

While social media connects people, it can also lead to cyberbullying and misinformation.

Many users feel pressure to portray a perfect life, which can create unrealistic expectations.

Social Media and Mental Health

Studies suggest that excessive social media use can contribute to anxiety and depression.

Taking regular breaks from social media can help maintain a healthy balance.
